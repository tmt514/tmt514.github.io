title: "Orthogonal Vectors"
description: |
    Given two sets $A, B\subseteq \set{0, 1}^d$, where $|A|=|B|=N$ and $d=\Theta(\log N)$. Then there exists $a\in A$ and $b\in B$ so that $\langle a, b\rangle = 0$.
